About Robotics

Robotics is a branch of engineering that involves the conception, design, manufacture, and operation of robots. This field overlaps with electronics, computer science, artificial intelligence, mechatronics, nanotechnology, and bioengineering. Robots can be operated by remote control, automatically by themselves, or a combination of both. NASA uses these robots to navigate planets and the universe. Spacecrafts that explore worlds are all robotic, as well as their tech inside their space crafts.
